How it works

From winner capture to shipping, all in one flow.

This is the fastest way to understand what SwiftSale does.

1

Capture winners live

As buyers win, SwiftSale captures usernames instantly during your show so you do not lose track.

2

Assign bins automatically

Each buyer gets a bin number. A bin is simply the place where that buyer’s order goes while you organize and pack.

3

Prep fulfillment faster

Use the BIN board, winner history, buyer tracking, exports, and label annotation to make your post-show packing process cleaner and faster.

4

Keep buyers engaged

Run built-in games and live chat output features like Bingo, Bin Bonanza, Prize Vault, and hype messages without breaking your flow.

FAQ

Questions first-time visitors usually ask.

Clear answers in plain English.

What is a bin?

A bin is simply the spot where a buyer’s order goes while you are organizing and packing.

Do I use SwiftSale during the live show?

Yes. SwiftSale helps you capture winners, assign bins, and run engagement features while the show is happening.

Does it also help after the show?

Yes. That is one of the biggest benefits. Your bins, buyer history, winners, and labels are already more organized for shipping.

Does SwiftSale print shipping labels?

SwiftSale helps annotate and organize labels so fulfillment is easier and faster to follow.

Why are games included?

Games like Bingo, Bin Bonanza, and Prize Vault help make your stream more fun, memorable, and interactive for buyers.

What does it run on?

The current production version is for Windows desktop.
